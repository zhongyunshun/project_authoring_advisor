#### Based on the "2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP" document, the defined roles and responsibilities of stakeholders involved in the Class Environmental Assessment for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project are:

1. **Proponent**: The proponent is responsible for:
	* Providing information on the project.
	* Identifying potential environmental effects.
	* Developing mitigation measures to address these effects.
2. **Public Consultation Participants** (e.g., local residents, business owners):
	* To provide feedback and comments on the proposed project.
3. **Reviewing Agencies**: The reviewing agencies are responsible for:
	* Reviewing the Class Environmental Assessment report.
	* Providing comments and recommendations to the proponent.
4. **Minister Responsible**: The Minister responsible is responsible for:
	* Making a decision on whether to approve or refuse the project based on the Class Environmental Assessment report.

Please note that the specific roles and responsibilities may vary depending on the jurisdiction and the specific requirements of the Class Environmental Assessment process.
