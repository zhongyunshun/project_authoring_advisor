#### Based on the "HBPE Project Brief - Phase I", it appears that TRCA (Toronto and Region Conservation) and the City of Toronto have the following roles and responsibilities in the maintenance and administration of Humber Bay Park East:

**TRCA:**

1. **Project Manager:** TRCA is responsible for managing the project, including coordinating with stakeholders, developing project schedules, and monitoring progress.
2. **Park Design and Planning:** TRCA will be involved in designing and planning the park's layout, trails, and other features.
3. **Environmental Assessment and Compliance:** As a conservation authority, TRCA has expertise in environmental assessment and compliance. They will ensure that the project meets all relevant environmental regulations.

**City of Toronto:**

1. **Stakeholder Engagement:** The City of Toronto will engage with stakeholders, including local residents, community groups, and other stakeholders.
2. **Park Maintenance and Operations:** The City of Toronto is responsible for maintaining and operating the park, including mowing, pruning, and other maintenance activities.
3. **Project Funding and Administration:** The City of Toronto will be responsible for administering project funding, including coordinating with funders, managing project budgets, and ensuring compliance with relevant regulations.

By working together, TRCA and the City of Toronto can ensure that Humber Bay Park East is maintained and administered in a way that meets the needs of all stakeholders.
