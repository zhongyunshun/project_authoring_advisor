#### Roles and Responsibilities of Stakeholders in the Class Environmental Assessment for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project

The roles and responsibilities of stakeholders involved in the Class Environmental Assessment for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project can be summarized as follows:

**Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (TRCA)**

* Conducts a Class EA assessment to evaluate potential environmental effects of the project.
* Prepares a Project Plan that outlines the scope, timeline, and budget for the project.
* Submits the Project Plan to the Minister of the Environment for review and approval.

**Minister of the Environment**

* Reviews and approves the Project Plan submitted by TRCA.
* Provides guidance and direction to TRCA on the implementation of the project.

**Public and Stakeholders**

* Provide input and comments during the Class EA assessment process.
* Review and comment on the Project Plan before its submission to the Minister of the Environment.

These stakeholders play critical roles in ensuring that the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project is implemented in a responsible and sustainable manner.
