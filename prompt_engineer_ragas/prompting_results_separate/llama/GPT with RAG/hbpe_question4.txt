1.  **TRCA's Role:** The Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (TRCA) is the lead agency responsible for the planning, management, and maintenance of Humber Bay Park East.

3.  **Collaboration between TRCA and the City of Toronto:** Both agencies work together to ensure that the maintenance and administration of Humber Bay Park East are carried out in a coordinated and effective manner.
