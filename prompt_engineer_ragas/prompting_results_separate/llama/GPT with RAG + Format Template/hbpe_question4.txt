#### Can you elaborate on the roles and responsibilities of TRCA and the City of Toronto in the maintenance and administration of Humber Bay Park East as described in the "HBPE Project Brief - Phase I"? ####

According to the "HBPE Project Brief - Phase I", the roles and responsibilities of TRCA and the City of Toronto in the maintenance and administration of Humber Bay Park East are as follows:

*   TRCA is responsible for:
    *   Retaining Shoreplan Engineering Limited (Shoreplan) for engineering services in support of the Humber Bay Park East Major Maintenance Project.
    *   Requesting detailed designs for the protection and maintenance of this stretch of shoreline.
    *   Collaborating with DFO, City of Toronto, and Shoreplan regarding the development of aquatic habitat features for the design.
*   The City of Toronto is responsible for:
    *   Maintaining the park's infrastructure, including trails, beach, and Air India 182 Memorial.
    *   Collaborating with TRCA, DFO, and Shoreplan regarding the development of aquatic habitat features for the design.

Please note that these roles and responsibilities may be subject to change based on further information or updates.
