#### What sustainable development practices are being implemented in the Humber Bay Park East Major Maintenance Project to ensure long-term environmental conservation? ####

Based on the retrieved passages, it appears that several sustainable development practices are being implemented in the Humber Bay Park East Major Maintenance Project.

1. **Minimizing disturbance of native material**: The protection structures were designed to minimize cutting and disturbing of native material (Document 17).
2. **Restoration plantings**: Site restoration will be limited to regrading and seeding for Phase I, with full Restoration Plan part of the detailed designs for the final phase (Document 22).
3. **Climate change considerations**: The project is expected to undergo climate change impacts, including water level fluctuations and storm frequency/intensity changes (Document 15).

These sustainable development practices are being implemented to ensure long-term environmental conservation in the Humber Bay Park East Major Maintenance Project.
