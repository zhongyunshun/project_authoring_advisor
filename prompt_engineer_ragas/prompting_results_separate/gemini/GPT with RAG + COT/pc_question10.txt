The "2021-09-29 Peacham Cres Project Brief" addresses potential impacts on biodiversity, particularly concerning bat species and butternut trees, as follows:

*   **Butternut Trees:** A Category 2 butternut tree has been identified within the project area, generally located along the proposed access route. The document references TRCA’s 2018-12-05 Biological Inventory Memo for more information. (Document 1) A terrestrial biological survey of the natural area within and surrounding the Project area was undertaken by TRCA’s Environmental Monitoring and Data Management (EMDM) in 2018 to inventory existing flora, fauna (Document 11)
*   **Bat Species:** Bat habitat is present within the project, and appropriate compensation will be coordinated by TRCA’s ERM Project staff in accordance with the Ontario Ministry of Environment, Conservation, and Parks (MECP) Bat Survey Protocol. (Document 8)
