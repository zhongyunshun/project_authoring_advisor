#### The "HBPE Project Brief - Phase I" outlines specific roles:

*   **TRCA (Toronto and Region Conservation Authority):** Owns Humber Bay Park East (Document 20). TRCA Archaeology Staff conducted a Stage 1 Archaeological Assessment in 2017-2018 (Document 19). Discussions are underway between TRCA, DFO, City of Toronto, and Shoreplan regarding the development of aquatic habitat features (Document 22).

*   **City of Toronto:** Discussions are underway between TRCA, DFO, City of Toronto, and Shoreplan regarding the development of aquatic habitat features (Document 22).

**In summary:** TRCA owns and manages the park and collaborates with the City of Toronto and other organizations on specific projects and features within the park.
