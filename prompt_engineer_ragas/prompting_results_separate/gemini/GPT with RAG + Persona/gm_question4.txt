Based on the provided documents, here's a breakdown of the stakeholder roles in the German Mills Settlers Park Sanitary Infrastructure Protection Project:

*   **Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (TRCA):** TRCA is a key partner in the project. Their roles include:
    *   Erosion Risk Management (ERM).
    *   Undertaking sanitary infrastructure protection works.
    *   Monitoring at-risk sanitary sites along German Mills Creek through the Infrastructure Hazard Monitoring Program (IHMP) since 2011.
    *   Working in partnership with the Regional Municipality of York.
    *   Completing the Schedule B Municipal Class Environmental Assessment (MCEA) Project File.

*   **Regional Municipality of York (the "Region"):** York Region Environmental Services partners with TRCA to undertake sanitary infrastructure protection works.

*   **Engineering Firms (e.g., GRECK and Associates Limited):** These firms are contracted to provide technical expertise and conduct assessments. Their roles include:
    *   Evaluating alternatives for sites.
    *   Providing cost estimates.

*   **Local Municipalities (City of Markham):** The project is located within the City of Markham.

*   **First Nations:** TRCA engages with First Nations.
