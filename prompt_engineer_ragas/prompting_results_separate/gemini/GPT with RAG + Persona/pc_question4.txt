Based on the provided documents from the "2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP", here's information regarding roles and responsibilities related to the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project:

*   **Conservation Authorities:** Under the Conservation Authorities Act, they have a mandate to reduce risk to life and property (Document 1).
*   **Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (TRCA):** TRCA is the lead authority responsible for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project, including undertaking studies and assessments to evaluate the project site and developing remedial solutions (Documents 2, 12, and 16). TRCA gratefully acknowledges contributions to the project from Alistair Jolly and Alvina Tam (Document 12).
*   **Minister of the Environment:** The Conservation Authority refers requests to the Minister of the Environment for a decision (Document 9).
*   **Public:** Public notification and consultation occurred, starting with the publication of a Notice of Intent (Document 3). Notices were sent to community groups, homeowners near the project site, and Indigenous communities (Document 3). The Conservation Authority submits an assessment upon project completion, commenting on the effectiveness of the Class EA process for meeting public concerns (Document 9).
