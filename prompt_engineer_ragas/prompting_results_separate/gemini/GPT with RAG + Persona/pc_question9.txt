Based on the provided documents, here's information regarding Indigenous community engagement for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project:

*   **Engagement Process:** Notices regarding the project were sent to Indigenous communities as part of the Class EA process, subsequent to the publication of the Notice of Intent on November 22, 2018 (Document 2). A full record of Indigenous community consultation, including copies of all correspondence, can be found in Appendix D of the Record of Indigenous Engagement for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project (Document 1).

*   **Indigenous Communities Consulted:** The following Indigenous communities were consulted (Document 3):

    *   Beausoleil First Nation
    *   Chippewas of Georgina Island First Nation
    *   Chippewas of Rama-Mnjikaning First Nation
    *   Huron-Wendat Nation
    *   Coordinator for the Williams Treaties First Nations
    *   Curve Lake First Nation
    *   Hiawatha First Nation
    *   Mississaugas of Alderville First Nation
    *   Mississaugas of the Credit First Nation

*   **Outcomes:** The documents state that there were no major concerns raised about the project (Document 1).
