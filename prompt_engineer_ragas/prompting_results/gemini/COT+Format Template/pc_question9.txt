Query: #### How were Indigenous communities engaged in the planning and decision-making processes for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project, and what outcomes resulted from this engagement? ####
Answer: "Indigenous communities were engaged in the planning and decision-making processes for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project. According to the documents:

*   **Notification:** Notices were sent to Indigenous communities regarding the project (Document 2).
*   **Consultation:** A full record of Indigenous community consultation, including copies of all correspondence, can be found in the Record of Indigenous Engagement for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project in Appendix D (Document 1).
*   **Specific Nations Involved:** The following First Nations were consulted: Beausoleil First Nation, Chippewas of Georgina Island First Nation, Chippewas of Rama-Mnjikaning First Nation, Huron-Wendat Nation, Coordinator for the Williams Treaties First Nations, Curve Lake First Nation, Hiawatha First Nation, Mississaugas of Alderville First Nation, and Mississaugas of the Credit First Nation (Document 3).
*   **Summary of Consultation:** Table 4 summarizes the indigenous group consultation for the project. Source: TRCA, 2018 (Document 19)

*   **Outcomes:** No major concerns were raised about this project (Document 1).
