#### In the German Mills Settlers Park Sanitary Infrastructure Protection Project, what are the specific roles of the various stakeholders, including TRCA, local municipalities, and engineering firms, in ensuring project success? ####
Based on the provided documents, here's a breakdown of the stakeholder roles in the German Mills Settlers Park Sanitary Infrastructure Protection Project:

*   **Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (TRCA):** TRCA plays a central role in the project, including Erosion Risk Management (ERM). TRCA is responsible for project management, environmental monitoring, and Indigenous engagement, and coordinating with other stakeholders. [Document 2, 3, 9, 20, 22]
*   **York Region Environmental Services (the “Region”):** The Region partners with TRCA in preparing sanitary infrastructure protection works. [Document 2, 9, 20]
*   **Local Municipalities:** The project is located within the City of Markham. [Document 20]
*   **Engineering Firms:** GRECK and Associates Limited is involved in the Schedule B Municipal Class Environmental Assessment, evaluation of alternatives, and providing cost estimates. [Document 4, 5, 6, 7, 10, 11, 12, 13, 15, 17, 18, 19]
