The "HBPE Project Brief - Phase I" outlines the roles of TRCA and the City of Toronto in the Humber Bay Park East Shoreline Maintenance Project. Humber Bay Park East is a public park owned by TRCA (Toronto and Region Conservation Authority). Discussions are underway between TRCA, DFO (Fisheries and Oceans Canada), City of Toronto, and Shoreplan regarding the development of aquatic habitat features for the design (HBPE Project Brief - Phase I.pdf, page 30; page 6).
