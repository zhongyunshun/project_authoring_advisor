The TRCA engaged with Indigenous communities during the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project. Here's what the documents reveal:

*   **Notification:** Notices regarding the project were sent to Indigenous communities (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 48).
*   **Consultation:** A full record of Indigenous community consultation, including correspondence, is available in Appendix D of the "Record of Indigenous Engagement for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project" (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 50).
*   **Specific Nations:** The following Indigenous nations were consulted (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 50):

    *   Beausoleil First Nation
    *   Chippewas of Georgina Island First Nation
    *   Chippewas of Rama-Mnjikaning First Nation
    *   Huron-Wendat Nation
    *   Coordinator for the Williams Treaties First Nations
    *   Curve Lake First Nation
    *   Hiawatha First Nation
    *   Mississaugas of Alderville First Nation
    *   Mississaugas of the Credit First Nation
*   **Concerns:** The documents indicate that there were no major concerns raised about the project (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 50).
