The documents provide information on Indigenous community engagement for the Peacham Crescent Slope Stabilization Project. Here's a summary:

*   **Engagement Process:** Notices regarding the project were sent to Indigenous communities (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 48). A full record of Indigenous community consultation, including copies of all correspondence, can be found in Appendix D of the Record of Indigenous Engagement (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 50).
*   **List of Indigenous Communities Consulted:** The following Indigenous communities were consulted (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 50):

    *   Beausoleil First Nation
    *   Chippewas of Georgina Island First Nation
    *   Chippewas of Rama-Mnjikaning First Nation
    *   Huron-Wendat Nation
    *   Coordinator for the Williams Treaties First Nations
    *   Curve Lake First Nation
    *   Hiawatha First Nation
    *   Mississaugas of Alderville First Nation
    *   Mississaugas of the Credit First Nation
*   **Outcomes:** No major concerns were raised about the project (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 50).

For more detailed information, consult the Record of Indigenous Engagement in Appendix D (2019-09-26 Peacham Class EA PP.pdf, page 50).
